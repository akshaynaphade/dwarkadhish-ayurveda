Our Story

Dr. Tanmay Yeole

B.A.M.S. Ayurveda

A Journey Back to Authenticity

"During my years of practice, I saw a disconnect. Patients were seeking natural wellness, but the market offered a confusing choice: either clinically pure but soulless products, or beautifully packaged luxury that lacked true Ayurvedic authenticity. I knew there had to be a better way to bring genuine, time-tested rituals to people who were looking for them."

"Dwarkadhish Ayurveda was born from a desire to bridge this gap. Inspired by the sacred purity of the Dwarka region , our mission is to deliver formulations that are not only effective but a complete sensorial experience. It’s about feeling the rich & authentic formulations. We call this 'Transparent Luxury' —our promise to you: pure, potent, and honest skincare from a land steeped in divine wellness."
